Flexible boxes with flex-grow property

Want to have some boxes that are always a little bit bigger than the rest, but still has the flexibility of the equal sized boxes.
Look no further. Boxes with the flex-grow property used are just the thing for you. Starting from .box-flex-1 and going up to .box-flex-12. 

<div class="row">
  <div class="box-flex-1"></div>
  <div class="box-flex-2"></div>
  <div class="box-flex-3"></div>
</div>
